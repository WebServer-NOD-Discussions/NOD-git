Repurposing approved drugs

Welcome to the New use of Old Drugs server. The NOD server can be efficiently used for querying FDA approved drugs against your protein or the proteome of your organism of interest. Our lab has a repute when it comes to identifying repurposed chemical compounds against proteins implicated in serious disorders: like TB, Malaria, Candida, Sars-Cov2.

About NOD

Repurposing drugs is not a new concept. It has a long successful history since 1967 when Novartis launched its first repurposed drugs against HIV.
It is a "universal strategy" for neglected diseases due to
1) reduced number of required clinical trial steps could reduce the time and costs for the medicine to reach market,
2) existing pharmaceutical supply chains could facilitate "formulation and distribution" of the drug,
3) known possibility of combining with other drugs could allow more effective treatment,
4) the repositioning could facilitate the discovery of "new mechanisms of action for old drugs and new classes of medicines"
5) the removal of “activation barriers” of early research stages can enable the project to advance rapidly into disease-oriented research.
Following are examples of certain compounds that have been favorable for repurposing.

  • FDA approved drug molecules
  • DNA aptamers
  • Natural and engineered peptides

We can also repurpose FDA approved drugs to which one particular microorganism has become resistant against another strain or type of organism.

Frequently Asked Questions

How should I decide the mode of operation?

  • What do you mean by the different mode of operations [MODE-1]?

    The NOD server provides the user with two basic modes of functioning.
    MODE:1- The user shall select to operate in mode-1 when the input is generally defined.
    For example:
    1) a complete proteome of an infectious organism, like Plasmodium falciparum. The input value in such a case shall be the taxonomic id for the organism.
    2) If you know multiple proteins from same or different organisms that are potential targets for drugs. Compile them in a multi-fasta file and upload it in mode-1


    The server will run in Mode-1 and output a table that would include the essential details about the drugs and external links for complete details.

  • What do you mean by the different mode of operations [MODE-2]?

    MODE:2 The user shall select to operate in mode-2 when the input is specifically defined. Thus the mode-2 can also be termed as an expert mode.
